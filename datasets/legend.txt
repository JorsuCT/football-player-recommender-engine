Player: Full name of the football player.
Age: Current age of the player.
Positions: Specific roles or areas on the pitch where the player operates (e.g., ST, CM, CB, GK).
Team: The football club the player currently represents.
League: The primary competition or division in which the player's team participates.
Foot: The dominant or preferred foot of the player (Left, Right, or Both).
Real_Salary: The actual financial compensation or wage the player earns.
Market_Value: The estimated financial value of the player in the transfer market.
Goals: Total number of goals scored by the player.
Expected Goals (xG): A statistical metric indicating the number of goals a player was expected to score based on the quality and probability of the chances they received.
Successful Dribbles: The number of times the player successfully bypassed an opponent while retaining possession of the ball.
Tackles: Total number of times the player successfully dispossessed an opposing player.
Assists: The number of passes provided by the player that directly resulted in a goal.
Accurate Passes Percentage: The ratio of completed passes to total attempted passes, expressed as a percentage.
Rating: An overall statistical score evaluating the player's performance during matches.
Big Chances Missed: The number of clear, high-probability goal-scoring opportunities that the player failed to convert.
Total Shots: The total count of goal attempts made by the player, including those on target, off target, and blocked.
Goal Conversion Percentage: The percentage of the player's total shots that successfully resulted in a goal.
Interceptions: The number of times the player anticipated and successfully intercepted an opponent's pass.
Clearances: The number of times the player kicked or headed the ball out of a defensive zone to relieve opposition pressure.
Error Lead To Goal: The number of individual defensive mistakes made by the player that directly resulted in the opposing team scoring.
Outfielder Blocks: The number of times a non-goalkeeper physically blocked an opponent's shot, cross, or pass.
Big Chances Created: The number of highly favorable goal-scoring opportunities the player generated for their teammates.
Accurate Passes: The total count of passes successfully delivered to a teammate.
Key Passes: The number of passes that directly led to a shot on goal by a teammate, without necessarily resulting in an assist.
Saves: The total number of shots on target successfully stopped by the goalkeeper.
Clean Sheet: The number of matches in which the player or goalkeeper completed the game without their team conceding a goal.
Penalty Save: The number of penalty kicks successfully stopped by the goalkeeper.
Saved Shots From Inside The Box: The number of saves made by the goalkeeper specifically from shots taken within the 18-yard penalty area.
Runs Out: The number of times the goalkeeper successfully rushed off their goal line to intercept a pass, collect a loose ball, or challenge an advancing attacker.
